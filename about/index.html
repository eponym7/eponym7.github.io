Stephen Ross

I’m a Canadian philosophy student nearing the end of my PhD. I’m also a classical musician who plays the piano.

This website is part calling card, part oasis for serious leisure (mainly reading and writing).

What will you write about?

There will be two streams: philosophy, and an eclectic hodgepodge of my other interests.

The philosophy stream will record thoughts which aren’t quite publishable but I still find worth sharing. I’m interested in questions about inquiry, logic and history, digested with the help of the philosophy of science and pragmatism.

The eclectic stream might include music, history, literature, games, … A friend of mine described it best:

This is gonna sound corny and hippie as all hell but I think there’s something to say for a travelogue of the mind, dude. I think it would be super far out for people to document their academic explorations.
